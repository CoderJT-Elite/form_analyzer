Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

Waitlist Data

When you submit the waitlist form, we collect your email address so we can contact you about beta access and product updates. We value your inbox and will never spam you.

How Data Is Processed

Waitlist submissions are processed securely by Formspree. Please review Formspree’s privacy policy for details on their industry-standard data handling.

App Privacy

The FormAnalyzer mobile app processes pose and camera data entirely on-device. No video or biometric data is ever uploaded to our servers. Your workout history remains private, stored locally on your device.

Contact

For privacy requests or questions, please contact the project maintainers through our official channels.
